### Filling

* 1 can peeled tomotoes
* 2-3 bunches of Scallions
* Grated parmesaen / romano to taste
* 1 - 1 1/2 Tbs Olive Oil
* Plain Bread Crumbs
* Pepper
* 1 onion Wedged

* Thinly sliced Silver tip
