* 1 lb Ground Beef
* 3/4 cup Bread Crumbs
* 3/4 cup Milk
* 1 egg, beaten
* 1/4 cup minced Onion
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/4 tsp each
    * pepper
    * celery salt
    * garlic salt
    * dry mustard
    * sage
* 1 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce

Mix all ingredients thoroughly

Spread mixture in pan and bake in 350 degree oven for 1 hour 15 minutes
